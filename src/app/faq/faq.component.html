Frequently Asked Questions:


Q: What is NE Architectural?

A: NE Architectural is an app designed to help you find places of architectural significance in the North East of England!
Q: How can I use NE Architectural?

A: All you need is a mobile phone with Android 4.1 or later and Google Maps installed!
Q: Is NE Architectural free?

A: Yes it is!
Q: Can I use NE Architectural anywhere?

A: It will work anywhere, but only provides information about places in the North East of England.
Q: Do you store my location history?

A: We do not store any information about your location, it is all handled by Google.
Q: What is the Timeline?

A: The timeline is a feature which allows you to see locations in a different way than on the map, ordering them by the date they were created!
Q: Can I review locations I have visited?

A: If you liked a location you visited, you can press the heart on the search page to like it!
Q: Do I need an internet connection to use NE Architectural?

A: You will need a Wi-Fi or mobile data connection for your location to update accurately, but all other features will work without a connection!

Troubleshooting


What do I do if my map does not show my location?

If this occurs, ensure that you have allowed the app to use your location in your device settings.
Nothing happens when I click "Take me there".

Please ensure that you have Google Maps installed on your device (it should be installed by default).
The text is too small on the screen.

You can change the font size for the whole app in the settings page.
The app crashes sometimes.

Please email us at NEArchitecturalTeam6@gmail.com with details of what you were doing before the crash.
